Top Five Things to Do in Medellín, Colombia

  1. Ride the cable car to Parque Arví
  2. Take a walking tour in Comuna 13
  3. Visit the Botero Plaza and Museum
  4. Explore the gardens at Parque Explora
  5. Experience the vibrant nightlife in El Poblado

Top Five Things to Do in Palomino, Colombia

  1. Relax on the pristine beaches
  2. Go tubing down the Palomino River
  3. Take a hike to the nearby Sierra Nevada mountains
  4. Visit the indigenous villages
  5. Enjoy fresh seafood at beachfront restaurants
